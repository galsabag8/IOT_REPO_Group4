Quick Guide

Wand Mode

1. Enable Wand Mode (Pink Button).
2. Load a MIDI file.
3. Hold wand steady to Calibrate.
4. Conduct vertically to play.

Visualizer

Click the Eye icon to see the 3D trail.
Press R to Recalibrate.

Replay & Record

Record: Arm recording before playing to save a CSV log.
Replay: Upload MIDI + CSV to watch previous sessions.
